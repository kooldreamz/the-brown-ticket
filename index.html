Welcome to The Brown Ticket !

TheBrownTicket is your ticket to the unseen India. Explore the unexplored and immerse yourself in the enchanting tales and ancient wonders of this captivating nation. A passionate team of artists, historians and seasoned travelers join you as guides sharing their wealth of knowledge and ensuring that every moment is infused with the essence of India's cultural legacy. Join us on an unforgettable odyssey through time with TheBrownTicket, where the echoes of history await your discovery.

What is "The Brown Ticket"?

A few decades ago, when travel and holidays were a once-a-year summer vacation activity, when trains were the mode of commute for a large part of India’s population - a rectangular “Brown Ticket” of Indian Railways used to be our escape from the mundane! Whether it meant another customary visit to dadi-nani house or a totally new destination - the joy of holding a Brown Ticket was unparalleled. And well, this was not the internet era of information-at-my-fingertips. The photographs of a dream destination were seen only on the glossy prints of magazines, often cut and pasted into scrapbooks, and the “top 10 things to do” blogs were non-existent. The Brown Ticket was our pass to an unimagined experience; the memories were saved in non-digital photo albums, to go over time and again, to showcase to family and friends, and to wait for another Brown Ticket to another dream. It is this nostalgic idea of old travel that we at the Brown Ticket aim to bring to our experiences.

Who We Are

We are a bunch of passionate travelers and history buffs. Some of us have worked the corporate ladder and know why, you need a break and some are pursuing their dream of travelling through time, so we know why you want your child to see what we have merely read in history books at school. We are explorers and would like to share our stories with you while listening to the experiences that bring you here. We manifest Great Experiences!
